## Context

This report documents, in an academic format, the activities and learnings achieved during a maintenance coordination mission in a GMP pharmaceutical environment subject to dual regulatory oversight by FDA and SwissMedic.

The mission took place during an active period of **SwissMedic and FDA validations**, covering the production of three publicly documented active pharmaceutical ingredients:
- **Lénalidomide** (Revlimid®)
- **Thalidomide** (Thalomid®)
- **Aprémilast** (Otezla®)

**Scope of responsibility:**
- Coordination of activities between **Utilities**, **Process** and **Calibration** departments
- Management of a maintenance budget of **~1.5M CHF**
- Supervision of a **warehouse keeper** and spare parts management
- Management under **Oracle CMMS/ERP** for assets and spare parts management
- Preventive maintenance strategy and planning of **periodic shutdowns**
