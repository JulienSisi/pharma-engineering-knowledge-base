## About the Role

The Infrastructure & Utilities Engineer Small Equipment role in a biologics CDMO like Lonza Visp is a **pivot position** at the intersection of three dimensions:

- **Engineering**: equipment reliability and lifecycle management
- **Quality**: GMP compliance, IQ/OQ/PQ, documentation, CAPA
- **Operations**: guaranteed availability for multi-product commercial production

This is not an entry-level position in the CDMO market — it's a SME (Subject Matter Expert) position with direct responsibility for the performance of lines in commercial production.

---

## Position Description (Summary)

**Main Responsibilities:**
- Ensure availability, reliability and performance of *small equipment*
- Supervise preventive and corrective maintenance strategies
- Act as SME in CAPEX projects (design → commissioning → qualification)
- Troubleshooting, RCA and CAPA implementation
- Full GMP compliance: SOPs, work instructions, deviations, change controls
- Support for audits and inspections (FDA, SwissMedic, clients)

**Required Profile:**
- ≥5 years experience in relevant field
- German and English required (bilingual site)
